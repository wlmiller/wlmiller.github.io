Projects

These are some of the projects I've worked on in my current position with Durwella.

Unplugged Viz

Unplugged Viz is a Xamarin.Forms component for complex scientific visualizations on iOS and Android.

A sample app utilizing Unpluged Viz is available on the App Store and Google Play, and full source for the demo app is available on GitHub.

geoSTATE

GeoScale's geoSTATE is a plug-in for the Petrel and SKUA-GOCAD geoscience platforms for comprehensive geomechanical modeling of pressures, stresses, and rock properties in subsurface volumes and along well paths.

The technologies used in the geoSTATE plugin include the Ocean SDK and WinForms (for the Petrel interface), Qt (for the SKUA-GOCAD interface), and C++ (for the shared core computational library).

KillSheet

KillSheet is a cross-platform (iOS and Android) mobile application that assists users in performing well kill operations.

It allows the user to enter all of the relevant physical chracteristics of the well and the kick, and calculates the required target pressures and volumes to safely kill the well.

KillSheet is a Xamarin.Forms app compiled for both iOS and Android devices.
